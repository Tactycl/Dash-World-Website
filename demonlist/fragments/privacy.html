Dash World Privacy Policy

    Effective: 21st April 2026

    1. Introduction

    This Privacy Policy outlines how Dash World ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares data about you as a user of our Service. It is essential to be aware that this Privacy Policy can change over time.

    2. Changes to this Policy

    We may amend this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced by stating the date of the last revision, when necessary, by sending you a direct notification on your account.

    3. The Data We Collect

    • Personal Information: Personal information that we collect when you sign up to create an account with us includes your Roblox ID, username, and any profile pictures.
    • Usage Information: We collect usage information based on how you engage with the Service. This involves levels created as well as scores you've entered.
    • Technical Information: We collect your IP address and time stamps.
    • Content Information: All content information you send to us, such as videos, levels, and records will be saved and made available to the public.

    4. How We Use Your Data

    • Service Delivery: To offer and sustain the Service, such as leaderboards creation, scoring, and account management.
    • Improvement & Development: To comprehend your usage pattern and enhance our service.
    • Communication: To keep you informed about your account via updates and administrative notices.
    • Security: To ensure the safety of our platform and guard against any fraudulent activity.

    5. Information Sharing and Release

    • Legal Requirements: Your information may be released where there is a legal obligation or in the process of complying with legal procedures.
    • Business Transactions: Your information may be transferred in case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

    6. Data Retention and Protection

    • Data Retention: Your personal data is retained for as long as it is needed for the purpose of this Privacy Policy or as provided by the applicable laws.
    • Security: We maintain high standards of data protection through industry-standard security measures. However, it is impossible to have complete safety when using online services.

    7. User Rights and Preferences

    • Access and Updates: You can access and modify your account details at any time using the account settings feature.
    • Requests: Should you require any additional information regarding your personal data, kindly reach out to us at the contact information provided.
