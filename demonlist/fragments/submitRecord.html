Rules

These are the rules to submit this form

Rules to settings:

  • You must not use Disable Shake on levels that require Shake to add difficulty.
  • You must not use Disable Gravity Effect at all, you are exempt from this rule if you are playing on 1.512 or earlier.
  • You must use Show Info Menu in your entire run. If you are playing before 1.6 you are exempt from this rule.
  • You are allowed to use fps beyond 240hz with an external FPS Unlocker, as it does not affect the in-game physics.

Rules to recording:

  • A full recording of the level completion from 0 to 100% is required, the end screen needs to be visible as well.
  • Your video must contain audible click sounds, if not present your record will be rejected.
  • Your video must be unedited from the start of the attempt until the end screen, if you edit any of these sections of the video your record will be rejected.
  • Your video must stay viewable that means, it needs to, for example on Youtube, stay Unlisted or Public. If your video ever goes private, we may remove your record from the list.

Rules to levels:

  • You must play the unedited and official version of the level, though you are also allowed to play with LDM enabled. You are also permitted to play one of the official LDMs provided by us and found on the level page in the list.
